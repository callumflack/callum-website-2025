## Predication before predicate

- **[The Kekulé Problem](https://nautil.us/the-kekul-problem-236574/), Cormac McCarthy.** <br/>The unconscious predates language and speaks in images.

- **[Driven by Compression Progress](https://arxiv.org/pdf/0812.4360v2.pdf), Jürgen Schmidhuber.** Beauty is compressibility; interest is its derivative.

- **[Enter The Supersensorium](https://thebaffler.com/salvos/enter-the-supersensorium-hoel), Erik Hoel.** Art is dreaming's cultural cousin.

- **[Fascinated to Presume: In Defense of Fiction](https://www.nybooks.com/articles/2019/10/24/zadie-smith-in-defense-of-fiction/), Zadie Smith.** <br/>Fiction presumes because no self is ever as fixed as the critics require.

- **[From Its Myriad Tips: Mushroom Brain](https://www.lrb.co.uk/the-paper/v43/n10/francis-gooding/from-its-myriad-tips), Francis Gooding.** <br/>Mycelium is cognition without a plan.

- **[Subjective Knowledge](http://www.incompleteideas.net/IncIdeas/SubjectiveKnowledge.html), Richard Sutton.** Knowledge is grounded in sensation and action.

## Guard what has no price

- **[Slack](https://thezvi.wordpress.com/2017/09/30/slack/), Zvi Mowshowitz.** Slack is absence of constraint. Guard it.

- **[Scenius, or Communal Genius](http://kk.org/thetechnium/scenius-or-comm/), Kevin Kelly.** Scenius is the genius of a scene. Destroyed by management, preserved by neglect.

- **[Geeks, MOPs, and sociopaths](https://meaningness.com/geeks-mops-sociopaths), David Chapman.** <br class="max-sm:hidden" />How scenes get colonised and hollowed out.

- **[Embracing the Improbable](https://kk.org/thetechnium/embracing-the-improbable/), Kevin Kelly.** Good futures are too specific to imagine.

- **[This is the Dream Time](https://www.overcomingbias.com/p/this-is-the-dream-timehtml), Robin Hanson.** We live in a brief, weird dreamtime.

## Elaborate what's already whole

- **[Deep Laziness](https://www.ribbonfarm.com/2018/04/06/deep-laziness/), Sarah Perry.** Elaborate the centres that already feel whole.

- **[Creative blocks](https://aeon.co/essays/how-close-are-we-to-creating-artificial-intelligence), David Deutsch.** Creativity is conjecture and criticism, not induction.

- **[Composers as Gardeners](https://www.edge.org/conversation/brian_eno-composers-as-gardeners), Brian Eno.** Seed the system. Surrender to its dynamics.

- **[The Playing Field](https://grahamduncan.blog/the-playing-field/), Graham Duncan.** Know exactly what game you're playing.

- **[Cognitive load is what matters](https://minds.md/zakirullin/cognitive), Artem Zakirullin.** Good code is cheap to hold in your head.

- **[Imagination Is Not Creativity](https://appliedcomplexity.substack.com/p/imagination-is-not-creativity), Applied Complexity.** Creativity is unfolding, not executing a plan.

- **[Is or Is Not The Opposite Stupid on its Face?](https://rogermartin.medium.com/is-or-is-not-the-opposite-stupid-on-its-face-ab635b9c5582), Roger Martin.** <br/>If the opposite isn't stupid, it's a real choice.

- **[Do The Easy Neglected Task](https://www.overcomingbias.com/p/do-the-easy-neglected-task), Robin Hanson.** The work that actually figures things out looks too easy to impress anyone, so nobody does it.

- **[You can't tell people anything](http://habitatchronicles.com/2004/04/you-cant-tell-people-anything/), Chip Morningstar.** People learn by doing, not by being told.

## Name the real thing

- **[My fiancee is pushing me away](https://thelastpsychiatrist.com/2012/02/my_fiancee_is_pushing_me_away.html), The Last Psychiatrist.** <br/>Introspection as elaborate defence.

- **[Beware the Casual Polymath](https://www.applieddivinitystudies.com/2020/09/28/polymath/), Applied Divinity Studies.** <br/>Polymaths launder status between domains.

- **[The World Face Up](https://covidianaesthetics.substack.com/p/the-world-face-up), Mónica Belevan.** <br class="max-sm:hidden" />Baroque had an outside. Hyperbaroque doesn't. You are now the stage.

- **[De Libero Occultorum](https://zermatist.substack.com/p/de-libero-occultorum), Zermatist.** <br/>Ad-tech is the new book of hidden things.

- **[Entropy is in the eye of the beholder](https://www.essentiafoundation.org/entropy-is-in-the-eye-of-the-beholder/reading/), Essentia Foundation.** <br/>Entropy is in the observer, not the system.

- **[What is Brand?](https://cra.mr/what-is-brand), David Cramer.** Brand is your values represented. Design isn't brand.

- **[The Web Is a Customer Service Medium](https://www.ftrain.com/wwic), Paul Ford.** Why wasn't I consulted?

- **[Context is that which is scarce](https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2022/02/context-is-that-which-is-scarce-2.html), Tyler Cowen.** Context is scarce. Teaching it is most of education.

## Orient to the centre

- **[Centering](http://gablog.cdh.ucla.edu/2017/10/centering/), Adam Katz.** MacIntyre's virtues reframed as centering: a practice is a disciplined way of taking turns at the centre.

- **[There Is No Economy but Only the Debt to the Center](https://anthropoetics.ucla.edu/ap2802/ap2802katzbaker/), Adam Katz & Zack Baker.** Exchange is tribute; every transaction re-enacts a debt to the centre.

- **[The Cosmic Chiasmus](https://www.thesymbolicworld.com/content/the-cosmic-chiasmus), Cormac Jones.** Reality is a fivefold chiasmus with Christ at the pivot. Everything is central by virtue of having a centre.

- **[Why Generative Anthropology?](https://x.com/centerstudy_/status/1891860674109673524), Peter Goldman.** Culture defers violence by representing it.

- **[On compositionality](https://julesh.com/2017/04/22/on-compositionality/), Jules Hedges.** The opposite of compositionality is emergence.

- **[SEELE 101](https://cityofplay.substack.com/p/seele-101-2), Outer Heaven.** The future is already here, in implication.
